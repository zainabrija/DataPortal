How It Works

Learn how to access and request data from Pakistan Bureau of Statistics

Step 1: Register

Create an account with your personal and organizational details to access the portal.

Step 2: Browse Data

Explore available datasets by category, search for specific data, or view recent additions.

Step 3: Request Data

Submit a detailed request for custom data or specific datasets not available publicly.

Step 4: Download

Download your requested data in your preferred format (CSV, Excel, PDF, JSON).

Available Data Types

  • Population and Demographics
  • Economic Indicators
  • Agricultural Statistics
  • Labor Force Data
  • Education Statistics
  • Health and Social Indicators
  • Infrastructure and Development

Frequently Asked Questions

Processing time varies depending on the complexity of your request. Simple requests may be processed within 3-5 business days, while complex custom data requests may take 2-4 weeks.

Basic datasets are available free of charge. Custom data processing and specialized requests may incur nominal fees to cover processing costs.
